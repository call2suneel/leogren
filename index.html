Who we are

LEOGREN PHARMA PVT LTD is a pharmaceutical company focused on the development and manufacturing of finished dosage forms. We support brand owners, distributors, and institutions with reliable, scalable production.

What we do

We offer third‑party manufacturing (loan licence/contract), product lifecycle support, packaging design coordination, and documentation for regulatory submissions where applicable.

How we deliver

Our operations emphasize robust vendor selection, validated processes, and multi‑stage quality checks to ensure every batch meets specification before release.
